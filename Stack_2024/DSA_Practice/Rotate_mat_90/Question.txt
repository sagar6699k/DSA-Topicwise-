Question: Rotate a matrix by 90 degree in clockwise direction without using any extra space
Discription:
Given a square matrix, turn it by 90 degrees in a clockwise direction without using any extra space.
Examples: 

Input:
1 2 3 
4 5 6
7 8 9  
Output:
7 4 1 
8 5 2
9 6 3

Input:
1 2
3 4
Output:
3 1
4 2
