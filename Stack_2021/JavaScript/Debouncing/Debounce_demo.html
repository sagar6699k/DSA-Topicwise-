Debouncing Demo :

-In debouncing, the event will be executed only after the last click, no matter how many time you click the button.

-It basically used to avoid the unneccessary action

Ex: lets consider you are building a shopping cart, there need to click on purchase button, imagine if user clicked on purchase button twice or thrice accidently , then in such case event will be executed that much of time. So to avoid such case Debouncing technique will be usefull.
